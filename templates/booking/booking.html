FAQ

Questions? We Have Answers.

How many channels are available?expand_more
Every rental includes 3 independent audio channels. Each channel broadcasts a different DJ or playlist — guests switch between them with one button press. LEDs glow red, green or blue to show which channel they're on.
Do I need to be in Lagos?expand_more
Not at all. We deliver nationwide. Standard pricing (shown in the calculator above) applies within Lagos state and includes an ₦80,000 service charge for transport and logistics. Outside Lagos state, pricing is different — message us with your headset count and location for a custom quote.
What happens if equipment is damaged?expand_more
Minor wear and tear is expected and covered. A lost or damaged headset or transmitter incurs a penalty of ₦67,000 per item, deducted from your refundable caution fee. The remaining deposit is sent back within 48 hours once all equipment is confirmed complete and undamaged.
How far in advance do I need to book?expand_more
We recommend at least 7 days for standard events and 14+ days for large festivals (150+ headsets). That said, message us even for last-minute requests — if the fleet is available, we'll make it work.
Is setup and a technician included?expand_more
Always. Delivery, on-site setup, a dedicated technician for the full event duration and post-event pickup are included in every single rental. There is no separate setup fee.
Can I connect my own DJ or playlist?expand_more
Yes — our transmitters accept any standard DJ mixer, laptop or phone output. Your DJ plugs straight into one of the three channels. We can also help configure the audio routing before the event starts.
