About us

Redefining Sound Experience in Nigeria

Decisive Sound NG specialises in premium silent headphone experiences for events across Nigeria — helping clients create exciting celebrations without the limitations of traditional loudspeakers.

Our story

Born from a love of noise-free celebrations

We started Decisive Sound NG after watching too many great parties get shut down by noise complaints or curfews. The fix wasn't a quieter party — it was headsets that let every guest control their own volume and pick their own channel.

Whether it's a rooftop gala or a backyard birthday, our team ensures every headset is a portal to a different world.
